Lasagne

Ingredients (serves 6)

  • 12 lasagne sheets
  • 500g minced beef (or half beef, half pork)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 800g canned tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50g butter
  • 50g plain flour
  • 600ml milk
  • 150g grated mozzarella
  • 50g grated parmesan

Method

  1. Cook the meat sauce

    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Add onion and garlic, sauté until soft.
    • Add minced meat and cook until browned.
    • Stir in tomato paste, canned tomatoes, oregano, and basil.
    • Simmer for 20–25 minutes until thickened. Season with salt and pepper.

  2. Make the béchamel sauce

    • Melt butter in a saucepan over medium heat.
    • Whisk in flour and cook for 1–2 minutes.
    • Gradually add milk, whisking constantly, until smooth and thickened.
    • Season with a pinch of salt and a little nutmeg if desired.

  3. Assemble and bake

    • Preheat oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Spread a layer of meat sauce in a baking dish, then a layer of lasagne sheets.
    • Add a layer of béchamel sauce, then sprinkle with mozzarella.
    • Repeat the layers, finishing with béchamel and parmesan on top.
    • Bake for 35–40 minutes until golden and bubbling.
    • Let rest 10 minutes before serving.
