Tiramisu

Ingredients (serves 6-8)

  • 250g mascarpone cheese
  • 3 large eggs, separated
  • 100g caster sugar
  • 200ml strong espresso or coffee, cooled
  • 2 tbsp coffee liqueur (optional)
  • 200g ladyfinger biscuits (savoiardi)
  • 2 tbsp unsweetened cocoa powder
  • Dark chocolate shavings (optional)

Method

  1. Prepare the cream

    • Beat egg yolks with sugar until pale and thick.
    • Fold in mascarpone until smooth.
    • In a separate bowl, whisk egg whites until stiff peaks form, then fold gently into the mascarpone mix.

  2. Assemble the tiramisu

    • Combine coffee and liqueur in a shallow dish.
    • Quickly dip each ladyfinger into the coffee (don't soak).
    • Arrange a layer of soaked biscuits in the bottom of a dish.
    • Spread half the mascarpone mixture over the biscuits.
    • Repeat with another layer of soaked biscuits and the remaining mascarpone.

  3. Chill and serve

    • Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours (overnight is best).
    • Dust generously with cocoa powder before serving.
    • Optional: top with dark chocolate shavings for extra richness.
